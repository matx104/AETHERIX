# AETHERIX Interview Preparation Guide

## EduQual Level 6 - Interview (30-40 minutes)

This guide prepares you for the technical interview portion of the oral examination.

## Interview Strategy

### Time Management
- 30-40 minutes for ~15-20 questions
- Average: 2 minutes per question
- Complex questions: 3-4 minutes
- Simple factual questions: 1 minute

### Response Structure (STAR-T)
1. **Situation/Setup**: Brief context (5 seconds)
2. **Technical**: Core technical answer (30-60 seconds)
3. **Application**: How it applies to AETHERIX (20 seconds)
4. **Reference**: Cite standards/papers if relevant (10 seconds)
5. **Transition**: Offer to elaborate or connect to related topics

## Practice Tips

1. **Know your numbers**: Light time, data rates, distances
2. **Understand trade-offs**: Why optical vs RF? Why RL vs CGR?
3. **Be ready to draw**: Network topology, protocol stack, RL agent
4. **Connect to standards**: CCSDS, RFC, IETF references
5. **Admit uncertainty**: "That's an interesting edge case..." is okay

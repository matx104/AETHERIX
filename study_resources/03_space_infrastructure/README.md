### Key Concepts to Master

1. **DSN Architecture** — Three complexes at 120° spacing (Goldstone, Madrid, Canberra)
2. **Lagrange Points** — L1-L5 positions, why L4/L5 are stable (Trojan points), why they're ideal for relays
3. **Areostationary Orbit** — Mars GEO equivalent at 17,032 km, 24.623 hr period
4. **Hybrid Optical/RF** — Optical for throughput, RF for reliability and weather
5. **5-Tier Topology** — Be able to draw and explain each tier
6. **Solar Conjunction Handling** — What happens, how AETHERIX routes around it
7. **LEO Laser Constellation** — 48 satellites for inter-satellite mesh

### Practice Questions

1. Why 3 DSN stations at 120° spacing? (30 seconds)
2. What is an areostationary orbit? How is it different from GEO? (1 minute)
3. Why use Lagrange point L4/L5 instead of L1/L2 for relays? (1 minute)
4. How does AETHERIX handle solar conjunction? (1 minute)
5. What are the tradeoffs of optical vs RF for deep space? (1 minute)

## The problem
Space radiation corrupts on-board computers. Between Earth and Mars a spacecraft
crosses the Van Allen belts, months of galactic cosmic rays (GCR), and possible
solar particle events — with no shielding atmosphere.

| Effect | What it does | Mitigation |
|--------|--------------|------------|
| **SEU** (Single Event Upset) | Single bit flip in memory/register | SECDED ECC |
| **MBU** (Multiple Bit Upset) | ≥2 adjacent bits flipped by one ion | Bit interleaving |
| **SEL** (Single Event Latchup) | Parasitic short — *destructive* | Current limiting + power-cycle |
| **TID** (Total Ionizing Dose) | Cumulative degradation (krad) | Rad-hard parts (RAD750) |

## Defense-in-depth stack
1. **TMR** — three replicas compute the same result; a majority voter masks any single faulty replica (≈3p² vs p).
2. **SECDED (39,32) ECC** — corrects 1 flipped bit, detects 2 (21.9% storage overhead).
3. **Memory scrubbing** — periodically rewrites corrected words *before* a second upset accumulates (the residual error is `P(≥2 upsets/word/scrub)`, a Poisson race).
4. **FDIR + watchdog** — detect → isolate → reset/reload golden image → SAFE-MODE after the recovery budget is exhausted.

## Headline numbers (AETHERIX model)
- **~37,000** raw bit upsets over a ~210-day, 512 Mbit GCR cruise → **~186 uncorrectable** (~0.9/day): **~200× protection**.
- **3,334×** TMR reliability gain at an assumed 1e-4/op logic-fault probability.
- **RAD750**: ~200 krad(Si) tolerance → **>2000× margin** on the Mars surface dose.

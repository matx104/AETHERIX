## Why 1550 nm optical — and not optical-only?

| Factor | 1550 nm optical | Ka-band RF |
|--------|-----------------|------------|
| **Throughput** | 2–200 Mbps downlink (DSOC-class) | 0.125–6 Mbps |
| **Advantage** | **10–100x** higher data rate | — |
| **Beam divergence** | Narrow (diffraction-limited λ/D) | Wider — easier pointing |
| **Weather** | Blocked by clouds (60–70% clear-sky per site) | Penetrates clouds (99%+ avail) |
| **Corona (conjunction)** | Severely degraded by solar-plasma scintillation | More robust at wide elongation |

**Decision:** *hybrid*. Optical carries the bulk science downlink; Ka-band RF
is the guaranteed-reliability fallback for emergency traffic and conjunction
periods. This mirrors NASA DSOC (optical) co-flying with the RF system on Psyche.

## Why a custom Python RL agent — and not ION-DTN's CGR?

| Criterion | Custom Q-learning agent | ION-DTN CGR (static schedule) |
|-----------|------------------------|-------------------------------|
| **Adaptivity** | Reacts to live link/buffer state in real time | Re-plans on schedule refresh (12+ min stale) |
| **Multi-objective** | Reward balances delivery, delay, hops, drops, energy | Optimises one metric (usually delay) |
| **Learning** | Policy improves over the 780-day synodic period | No learning from experience |
| **Federated** | Multi-agent Q-table aggregation across nodes | Centralised schedule |

**Decision:** *custom RL now, ION-DTN later (Phase 6)*. The custom agent was
built for rapid prototyping and flexible integration of Federated Q-Learning —
every Q-value is inspectable ("from node X, the best action when buffer is 80%
full is STORE"), which is essential for a research project that must justify
every decision. The reward function `R = α(delivery) − β(delay) − γ(hops) −
δ(drops) − ε(energy)` is validated here; Phase 6 plugs the learned policy into
ION-DTN's convergence layers for standards-compliant deployment.

## Why Q-tables before Deep Q-Networks (DQN)?

- **Interpretability** — every Q-value is human-auditable (DQN is a black box).
- **Rapid iteration** — Q-table trains in seconds; reward weights tune fast.
- **Right-sized state space** — 241 nodes with discretised state variables fit
  a Q-table. The DQN upgrade path (neural net + experience replay + target
  networks) is documented for the production transition.

**Bottom line:** every choice trades maximum theoretical performance for
*auditability and reproducibility* — exactly what a defence and a research
artefact require. `Code:` `src/routing/rl_agent.py`, `src/routing/training.py`.
